## üñºÔ∏è Photo Requirements

For best results with all templates:

- **Format**: JPG, JPEG, or PNG
- **Size**: Maximum 5MB
- **Dimensions**: Square photos work best (e.g., 500x500px)
- **Quality**: High resolution (at least 300x300px)
- **Background**: Plain or professional background recommended
- **Pose**: Professional headshot, facing camera
- **Clothing**: Business attire recommended
